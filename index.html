About Us

The Office of Student Success and Support Services at UDM is committed to supporting students academically, personally, and professionally. The office offers diverse services to help students from when they enter the school until graduation. The goal is to empower students to succeed during their time at UDM. The office provides resources that make a difference. The staff stands ready to assist in areas such as course selection or placement after studies. The school believes that every student can learn, and aims to assist students in achieving their best.
