About Us

The College of Engineering and Technology (CET) Local Student Council (LSC) is an organization managed by students dedicated to assisting CET students by serving as a bridge between administration and the student body. The LSC ensures that critical information, announcements, and changes from higher levels are efficiently transmitted to the CET community. The council also organizes departmental activities like as CET Week, welcoming parties, booths, and actively participates in University Week (U-week), all of which aim to improve the student experience and build a feeling of community. The LSC's website provides a simple platform for CET students to remain up to date, learn about upcoming events, and simply contact them with any problems or questions. This demonstrates the council's dedication to increasing communication and strengthening the department community.
